Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

Sree Collections ("we", "our", "us") operates the website at sreecollectionsknl.vercel.app. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you place an order with us.

1. Information We Collect

When you place an order, we collect:

  • Full name
  • Mobile phone number
  • Email address
  • Delivery address and pincode
  • Order details (product, quantity, amount)
  • Payment method (Cash on Delivery or Online)

For online payments, payment processing is handled by Razorpay. We do not store your card numbers, CVV, or UPI PIN. Razorpay's privacy policy applies to payment data.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To process and fulfil your orders
  • To contact you about your order status
  • To send order confirmation emails
  • To handle returns and refunds

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

3. Data Storage

Your order data is stored securely in our database (Supabase, hosted in the EU). Order data is retained for 2 years for business records purposes.

4. Payment Processing

Online payments are processed by Razorpay (razorpay.com). We only receive a payment confirmation ID — we never see your full card details. Razorpay is PCI DSS compliant.

5. Cookies

We use minimal browser storage (localStorage) to remember your cart items and theme preference. No tracking cookies or third-party advertising cookies are used.

6. Your Rights

You may request deletion of your personal data at any time by contacting us. We will delete your order history and personal details within 7 business days.
